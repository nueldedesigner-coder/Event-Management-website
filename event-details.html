About this event

Campus Beats Live Fest brings together student bands, DJs, and surprise guest performers for one night of live music on the Main Amphitheatre stage. Expect a full sound and lighting setup, food vendors on the lawn, and performances running from early evening into the night.

This is a fan-made, student-organized event. All performances are original sets or licensed covers by student and campus-affiliated performers.
